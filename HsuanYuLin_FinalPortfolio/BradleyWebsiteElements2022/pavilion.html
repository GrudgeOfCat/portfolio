Pavilion

For the final project, we created a pavilion in 3ds Max with all the skills learned in previous projects. In the project we added props and images in the background to create a more realistic effect. My original idea for this design was to create a pavilion with wood sticks and not a regular structure. The design of the pavilion was inspired by the shape of a whale. I later decided to place incorporate the beach background to better fitted with the whale design. I was satisfied with how the pavilion turned out. The goal of the pavilion is to let the tourists to walk through and take pictures along with the beautiful view of the ocean. Overall, this is my favorite project in this class, there isn’t much restrictions on the design and allowed me to put whatever come to my mind into design.
