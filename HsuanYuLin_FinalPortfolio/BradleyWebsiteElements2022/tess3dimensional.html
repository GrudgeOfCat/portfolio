Tessellation - Phase 3 - 3Dimensioal Wall

For the last phase of the Tessellation project, we moved into 3ds Max to create the piece. We first learned the basics of 3ds Max to help us modified our tessellation. I struggled with this project quite a bit in the beginning. Since my original piece fits together perfectly, a lot of modification I tried tends to mess up on how the pieces fits together. In the end I scaled down one side of each piece. I was satisfied with how it turned out after rendering. However, the 3D printed piece doesn’t come out with the same feel, I would like to have it printed out in a larger scale.
